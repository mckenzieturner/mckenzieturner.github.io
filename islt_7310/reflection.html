Reflection

A few things that I learned in my current internship are as follows. I have had great experience working in a semiconductor company and excited to see where it takes me.


Volatile organic compounds monitoring is a simple monitoring system that is used for air monitoring. I think that it is beneficial for every company because it goes along with the rules and regulations. It keeps the workers safe from inhaling any type of gas or particles that could be dangerous.


Respirator fit tests are the next informational project. I am learning about these to keep workers safe in their business. I learned how to properly fit an employee based on the type of respirator and the fit on their face. Each respirator has a different purpose, and some keep you safer than others. Emergency showers and eye wash stations are very important to any business and especially at this company.
